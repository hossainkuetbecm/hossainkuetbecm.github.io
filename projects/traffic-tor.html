Technical Documentation

Digital Traffic Management TOR for Chattogram City

TOR and technical documentation support for digital traffic management and intersection improvement.

Project Overview

This project involved Terms of Reference documentation for detail design consultancy scope related to digital traffic management and intersection improvement at Chattogram City. The documentation covered traffic signal development, lane marking, zebra crossing, parking/drop-off facilities and public transport stoppage components.

My Responsibilities

  • Prepared TOR document including project background, objectives, scope of services, location and intersection list.
  • Documented objectives to manage mechanized and non-mechanized vehicle movement.
  • Documented objectives to reduce traffic congestion, improve junction design and support automated traffic enforcement.
  • Included components such as traffic signal development, lane marking and zebra crossing.
  • Included parking spot, dropping zone and bus/car/rickshaw/CNG stoppage components.
  • Prepared documentation for 37 intersections under two phases of digital traffic management.

Work Scope

  • TOR preparation
  • Urban infrastructure documentation
  • Traffic signal and junction improvement scope
  • Intersection list documentation
  • Technical report formatting
