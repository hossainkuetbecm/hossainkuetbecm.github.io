Illumination & Circuit Load Calculation

Electrical illumination and load calculation document for building spaces and distribution board circuits.

Project Overview

This work involved illumination calculation and circuit current/power calculation documentation for building spaces and distribution board circuits. The deliverable was organized as a professional calculation document with tables and formulas.

My Responsibilities

  • Calculated room-wise area, standard illumination and total lumen requirement.
  • Prepared illumination calculation for entry, drawing room, child bed, common bath, dining space, master bed, balcony, bath and kitchen.
  • Prepared circuit current and power calculation for distribution board circuits.
  • Prepared load schedules for CFL, tube light, down light, fan, exhaust fan, 2-pin socket, 3-pin socket and wall bracket.
  • Calculated circuit-wise total wattage and maximum current for multiple sub-circuits and distribution boards.
  • Organized calculation sheet in a professional document format with tables and formulas.

Work Scope

  • Illumination calculation
  • Circuit current calculation
  • Power/load calculation
  • Distribution board load schedule
  • Professional calculation sheet formatting
