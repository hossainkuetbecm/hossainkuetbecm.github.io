Project Overview

This academic building project involved structural design support and drawing documentation for an RCC academic building with ground plus four/five-storied superstructure at West Chall, Anowara, Chattogram.

My Responsibilities

  • Performed structural analysis and design support for the academic building project.
  • Prepared structural drawing documentation for RCC framing requirements.
  • Prepared framing layout support.
  • Prepared column, beam and slab detail support.
  • Prepared foundation-related structural details.
  • Assisted in drawing coordination for architectural and structural requirements of academic building use.
