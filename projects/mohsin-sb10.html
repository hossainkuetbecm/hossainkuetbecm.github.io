Structural Project

Mr. Mohsin — SB+10 Storied Mixed-Use Building

RCC mixed-use building with semi-basement and ten-storied superstructure.

Project Overview

This project involved RCC structural analysis and design support for a semi-basement plus 10-storied mixed-use building at Soltgola, Bondor, Chattogram. The work focused on preparing a coordinated structural drawing package suitable for client/consultant submission and project documentation.

My Responsibilities

  • Performed structural analysis and design support for the RCC mixed-use building.
  • Prepared structural drawing package, drawing index and general structural notes.
  • Prepared site layout, column layout, column schedule and shear wall schedule.
  • Prepared pile layout, pile long section details and pile cap layout/details.
  • Prepared UGWR layout, septic tank layout, underground water reservoir details and septic tank details.
  • Prepared retaining wall layout/details, ramp details and stair details.
  • Prepared ground floor beam layout, 1st to 3rd floor beam layout and typical floor beam layout.
  • Prepared roof beam details, slab reinforcement details, roof beam/slab details, floor plans, roof top plan, OHWR layout and lift machine room details.

Work Scope

  • RCC structural design support
  • AutoCAD structural drafting
  • Structural drawing package preparation
  • Foundation, pile and pile cap details
  • Beam, slab, stair, roof and water reservoir details

Project Information

Year: September 2022

Location: Soltgola, Bondor, Chattogram

Client / Authority: Mr. Mohsin

Role: Assistant Engineer / Structural Designer / Drafting Support
