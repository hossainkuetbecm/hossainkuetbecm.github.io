Structural Project

SB+G+7.5 Storied Residential Building

RCC residential building with sub-basement/ground and multi-storied superstructure.

Project Overview

This project involved RCC structural analysis and design support for a residential building with sub-basement/ground and a multi-storied superstructure at Sangbadik Housing Residential Area, Chattogram. The work focused on drawing package preparation and technical coordination for submission.

My Responsibilities

  • Performed RCC structural analysis and design support for building components.
  • Prepared preliminary structural drawing package, drawing index and general structural notes.
  • Prepared site layout plan, column layout plan, column schedule, typical floor plan and column/shear wall layout.
  • Prepared foundation/pile layout, foundation details, pile/pile cap related details and long section details.
  • Prepared UGWR layout, retaining wall layout, septic tank layout, retaining wall details and septic tank details.
  • Prepared floor beam layout, floor beam details, slab reinforcement details and stair details.
  • Prepared roof beam/slab details and OHWR/lift machine room details.
  • Coordinated structural drawing set for client/consultant submission.

Work Scope

  • RCC structural design support
  • Structural drawing package
  • Column, beam and slab detailing
  • Foundation, pile and pile cap details
  • Water reservoir, retaining wall, septic tank and stair details

Project Information

Year: October 2022

Location: Plot No. 17, Sangbadik Housing R/A, Chattogram

Client / Authority: Mr. Md. Fakhruddin

Role: Assistant Engineer / Structural Designer / Drafting Support
